ABOUT

WELCOME TO CHARACTER SELECT

With the rise of hero based video games, finding a way to discover your new favorite character can be daunting. Sifting through half the roster can take time and dedicating time to a character or role you may not stick with can feel like time wasted.

Character Select aims to alleviate the stress and confusion of finding your new main by solving the problem before the problem starts by providing multiple surveys for a wide variety of games to help you narrow your focus and alleviate the stress of finding a new character.

DEVELOPERS

Nathan and Brendan are two recent college graduates with degrees and expertise in technology. With a shared love and passion for video games,. they set out to make a personality style test for determining a player's preference.

This project serves as a chance for them to collaborate not only as professionals, but as best friends looking to improve themselves as wellas each other.
